I'm Jason, a Magician

Hi! My name is Jason Fang, a professional magician based in Washington D.C., Maryland, and Virginia. With more than 10 years of experience as a professional magician, I have performed thousands of magic shows to people around the world. I always design different magic routines for different kinds of audiences and situations. My magic routines include card tricks, coin tricks, mind reading, Tarot divination, street magic, stage magic, comedy magic and so on.

While magic is meant for adults. In terms of entertaining kids, I do balloon animals and face painting for them. Also, there are some specific kid's magic routines for them.
